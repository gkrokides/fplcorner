About Us

We believe there are 3 pillars for succeeding at FPL and getting those green arrows consistently. Watching a lot of games, being on top of news and suspensions and correctly exploiting the data.

The quant part of FPL can be a bit overwhelming especially when you are not sure what you are looking for. What we try to do at FPLcorner.com, is simplify this process, trying to squeeze in all freely available data in a friendly graphical representation, that makes sense! Have a look at our Player Comparison and Discover Value tools and feel free to send us any comments or feedback.
